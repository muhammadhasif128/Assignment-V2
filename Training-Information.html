Training Information

Body Workout

Body Workout Includes:
1) Reverse lunge press – 10 reps
2) Front squats – 10 reps
3) Kettlebell swings – 10 reps
4) Thrusters – 10 reps
5) Sumo deadlift – 10 reps
6) Military press – 10 reps

[2 minute rest]
[Repeat 5 times]

Objectives Of Our Workout

Helps you at:
1) Improving your neck
- to sustain the high speeds through the corners
2) Building strong arms
3) Building strong legs
4) Building strong core
5) Building a strong mind
6) Building good stamina

[Ensures you are fit before entering a race car]
[Avoid avoidable injuries at ALL Costs]

2) Long Distance Cycling
3) Swimming
4) Running On Treadmill
5) Sprints
6) Rope Skipping

[Keep rotating through each exercises]
