Our Vision

To provide Singaporeans with a dream in racing in the highest open-wheel-single-seater a chance in landing a seat in the sport of F1!

All racing license will be provided from all the license class, step by step, to get to your final destination, F1.

We cater to your goals and needs here at
Hasif F1 Academy!

Our Mission

At Hasif F1 Academy, we have connections with Former F1 World Champions such as
Nico Rosberg, Jenson Button, Fernando Alonso who have agreed to coach those that sign up with us!

We have amazing facilities at KF1 Karting Circuit, to build your foundation well as well as friendly trainers that will make the environment such a friendly one to grow as a potential racing driver!

Our Values

We are dedicated to providing equal chances to everyone that signed up and treating every customer with the outmost respect.

We are always pushing the limits here, and we NEVER GIVE UP!

Lastly, we strive to achieve the Impossible!
